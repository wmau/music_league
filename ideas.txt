Song level
- Most hated (song with the lowest score)
- Most contentious (song with the most number of votes and yet the lowest score)
- Most beloved (song with the highest score)

Player level
- Dissenter: Person most often giving a negative vote to a highly placed song
- Sophisticated: Person with the highest number of genres liked
- Blabbermouth: Person with the most comments (or longest comments)
- The Stoic One: Person with the fewest comments
- Player relationships: Person who likes and dislikes your songs the most
